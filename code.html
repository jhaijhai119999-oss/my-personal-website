MY MEMORABLE SCHOOL COVERED COURT

Our school covered court is one of the most memorable places for me.

It is not just a place where students gather, but it is also where many important events and unforgettable memories are made. Throughout the years, our covered court has been the venue for flag ceremonies, intramurals, recognition programs, Christmas parties, school performances, and many other activities.

Every event held in our covered court brought happiness and excitement to me and my classmates. I always enjoyed cheering during sports competitions, watching performances, and celebrating achievements together with my friends and teachers. These moments helped strengthen our friendship and taught us the value of teamwork and unity.

Whenever I see our covered court, I remember the laughter, fun, and memories that we shared there. It is more than just a place for activities—it symbolizes friendship, school spirit, and experiences that I will always treasure. Even after I graduate, the memories created in our school's covered court will remain special in my heart.
